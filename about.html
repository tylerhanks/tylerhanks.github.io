About Me

"Always start your 'About Me' page with an inspirational quote." - Sun Tzu, Art of War

Hello, I am a fourth year CS PhD student at UF in Gainesville, Florida! I work on applied math, scientific computing, and optimization in Dr. James Fairbanks's GATAS Lab. Before that, I did my undergrad in CS at the University of South Florida, where I worked on software security and programming language theory with Dr. Jay Ligatti. My graduate research has given me the opportunity to travel all over the world, from Singapore to the UK, presenting my work and meeting cool people! My main non-academic hobbies include playing music, basketball, and videogames. I played drums for many years in a local alternative band called Infinite Eights. If you are looking for a drummer in Gainesville, hit me up! If you are looking to hire an experienced researcher who is passionate about all things math and computer science, also hit me up! I hope you have a wonderful day.
